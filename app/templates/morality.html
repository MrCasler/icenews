Why Documenting ICE Activity Matters

In an era of increasing surveillance and government overreach, documenting the actions of Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) has become a critical form of civic journalism.

The Threat of Totalitarian Control

When government agencies operate without transparency or accountability, the foundation of democracy erodes. History teaches us that unchecked power inevitably leads to abuse. The documentation of enforcement actions serves as a crucial check on power.

Every video, every witness account, every piece of evidence helps ensure that those in power cannot rewrite history or deny their actions. This is not about politics—it's about preserving the truth.

What You Can Do

  • Document safely – If you witness ICE activity, record from a safe distance. Know your rights.
  • Share responsibly – Protect identities of vulnerable individuals while exposing official actions.
  • Support local organizations – Connect with immigrant rights groups in your community.
