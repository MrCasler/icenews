Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025. Simple and transparent.

What we collect

We collect only what we need: your email (for login magic links), optional nickname, and any content you choose to upload (e.g. titles, descriptions, links). We do not sell your data.

How we use it

Your email is used to sign you in and to associate your uploads and downloads with your account. Uploaded content is shown in the public gallery and feed as described on the site. We use industry-standard practices to keep data secure.

AI and automation

We are transparent: This site uses AI (e.g. for development, content tools, and improvements). We do not hide that. Unlike some institutions, we believe you deserve to know when technology like AI is involved.

Cookies and sessions

We use a session cookie to keep you logged in. No tracking or advertising cookies.

Your rights

You can stop using the site at any time. If you want your data removed or have questions, contact us (e.g. via the contact method listed on the site or in the app).

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised policy.
